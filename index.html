Visualization Overview
This chloropleth map visualizes median household income. 5 US regions (Northeastern, Southern, Midwestern, Western, and California) are outlined by unique colors. Circles representing the median starting salary and median mid-career salaries of college graduates who attended school in the region have been overlaid on the map.

The purpose of this visualization is to explore whether graduating college in "wealthier" regions correlates with a higher starting and mid-career salary.
